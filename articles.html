How to Manage When a Sudden Fire Occurs

In the event of a sudden fire, it’s crucial to remain calm. Follow these steps:

1. Activate the nearest fire alarm.

2. Evacuate the building immediately.

3. Do not use elevators; use stairs instead.

4. If trapped, seal doors with clothing and signal for help.

Always prioritize safety and contact emergency services.

Understanding Different Types of Physical Trauma

Physical trauma can result from accidents, falls, or violence. Here’s how to handle it:

1. Assess the injury and seek immediate medical attention if severe.

2. For minor injuries, apply first aid and monitor the condition.

3. Know the signs of shock and how to manage it.

Being prepared can save lives.
